# Common Issues & Solutions

## Order Issues

### I haven't received my order confirmation

**Possible causes**:
- Email went to spam/junk folder
- Incorrect email address entered
- Order didn't complete successfully

**Solutions**:
1. Check spam/junk folder for email from orders@smartinsights.com
2. Log into your account and check order history
3. Search email for "Smart Insights" or order confirmation
4. If no order found, your payment may not have processed - try ordering again
5. Contact support with payment confirmation if charge appears

### My order is delayed

**Possible causes**:
- High demand period (holidays)
- Item backordered
- Shipping carrier delays
- Address verification issue

**Solutions**:
1. Check tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
2. Verify your shipping address is correct in order details
3. Contact carrier directly with tracking number
4. Allow 1-2 extra days during peak seasons
5. Contact support if package shows no movement for 5+ days

### I received the wrong item

**Steps to resolve**:
1. Do not discard the incorrect item
2. Take photos of the item received and shipping label
3. Go to your order and click "Report Issue"
4. Select "Wrong Item Received"
5. We'll send a prepaid return label and ship correct item

**Timeline**: Replacement shipped within 24 hours of report

### Item arrived damaged

**Steps to resolve**:
1. Take photos of damaged item and packaging
2. Report within 48 hours of delivery
3. Go to order > "Report Issue" > "Damaged Item"
4. Upload photos when prompted
5. Choose replacement or refund

**Note**: Keep damaged items until claim is resolved

## Account Issues

### I forgot my password

1. Click "Sign In" on homepage
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check email for reset link (valid 24 hours)
5. Create new password (minimum 8 characters)

**Tip**: Add smartinsights.com to your email contacts

### I can't log into my account

**Common causes and solutions**:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Wrong password | Use "Forgot Password" to reset |
| Account locked | Wait 30 minutes or contact support |
| Browser issue | Clear cache, try incognito mode |
| Two-factor auth | Check authenticator app or SMS |

### How do I update my email address?

1. Log into your account
2. Go to Account Settings > Profile
3. Click "Edit" next to email
4. Enter new email address
5. Verify via confirmation email
6. Old email will be notified of change

### How do I delete my account?

1. Log into your account
2. Go to Account Settings > Privacy
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm by entering password
5. Account deleted within 30 days

**Note**: Order history retained for 7 years per legal requirements

## Payment Issues

### My payment was declined

**Common reasons**:
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Billing address mismatch
- Bank fraud protection triggered

**Solutions**:
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Ensure billing address matches card statement
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank to authorize the transaction
5. Check if your card works for online purchases

### I was charged twice

**Don't worry** - this is usually a pending authorization, not a double charge.

1. Pending authorizations drop off within 3-5 business days
2. Check your statement after 5 days
3. If still showing two charges, contact support with:
   - Order number
   - Last 4 digits of card
   - Screenshot of statement

### How do I get a refund?

**Refund process**:
1. Initiate return through your order page
2. Ship item back using prepaid label
3. Refund processed within 2-3 days of receipt
4. Refund appears in 5-7 business days (credit card)

**Refund timeline by payment method**:
| Method | Processing Time |
|--------|-----------------|
| Credit Card | 5-7 business days |
| Debit Card | 5-10 business days |
| PayPal | 3-5 business days |
| Store Credit | Immediate |

## Shipping Issues

### How do I track my package?

1. Check shipping confirmation email for tracking link
2. Or log into account > Orders > View Order > Track Package
3. Click tracking number to see carrier updates

### Package shows delivered but I didn't receive it

**Steps**:
1. Check around your property (back door, garage, bushes)
2. Ask neighbors if they received it
3. Check with building management/front desk
4. Wait 24 hours (sometimes marked early)
5. Contact carrier with tracking number
6. Report to us after 48 hours if still missing

### Can I change my shipping address?

**Before shipped**: 
- Edit order within 1 hour of placing
- Contact support immediately if past 1 hour

**After shipped**:
- Contact carrier directly with tracking number
- Request hold at facility or redirect

### My package is stuck in transit

If no tracking updates for 5+ days:
1. Contact carrier directly first
2. Provide tracking number and request investigation
3. If carrier can't locate, contact our support
4. We'll file a claim and offer replacement/refund

## Product Issues

### Item doesn't match description

1. Compare with product page carefully
2. Check if variation (color/size) matches order
3. If genuine discrepancy, report via order page
4. Include photos showing difference
5. We'll arrange return with full refund

### How do I leave a product review?

1. Log into your account
2. Go to order history
3. Click "Write Review" next to purchased item
4. Rate 1-5 stars
5. Write your review (optional photos)
6. Submit - appears within 24 hours

### Product is out of stock

**Options**:
1. Click "Notify Me" to get restock alert
2. Check "Similar Products" recommendations
3. Add to wishlist for later
4. Contact support for estimated restock date

## Technical Issues

### Website not loading properly

1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable browser extensions
4. Check internet connection
5. Try incognito/private mode

### App crashing

1. Force close and reopen app
2. Check for app updates
3. Restart your device
4. Uninstall and reinstall app
5. Ensure sufficient storage space

### Can't add items to cart

1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check if item is in stock
4. Try removing and re-adding
5. Log out and log back in
