# Privacy Policy

## Introduction

Smart Insights Store ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, password, phone number
- **Billing Information**: Credit card details, billing address
- **Shipping Information**: Delivery address, delivery preferences
- **Communication**: Support tickets, emails, chat transcripts
- **Preferences**: Product preferences, notification settings

### Information We Collect Automatically

- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device ID
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, products viewed, search queries
- **Location Data**: IP address, general geographic location
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Process Orders**: Fulfill purchases, process payments, arrange shipping
2. **Customer Service**: Respond to inquiries, resolve issues, process returns
3. **Personalization**: Recommend products, customize your experience
4. **Marketing**: Send promotional emails (with your consent)
5. **Analytics**: Improve our website, products, and services
6. **Security**: Detect fraud, protect against unauthorized access

## Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

### Service Providers
- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Shipping carriers (DHL, FedEx, LocalCarrier)
- Email service providers
- Analytics providers

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

### Business Transfers
In case of merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
- PCI-DSS compliance for payment processing
- Regular security audits
- Employee access controls
- Data encryption at rest

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Essential | Site functionality | Session |
| Preference | Remember settings | 1 year |
| Analytics | Usage statistics | 2 years |
| Marketing | Personalized ads | 90 days |

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

## Data Retention

We retain your data for:

- Account information: Duration of account + 3 years
- Order history: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Support tickets: 3 years
- Analytics data: 2 years

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or website notice.
