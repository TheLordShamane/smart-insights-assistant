# Return Policy

## Overview

At Smart Insights Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return process.

## Return Window

- **Standard items**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Electronics**: 15 days from delivery date
- **Apparel**: 45 days from delivery date (unworn, tags attached)
- **Grocery items**: Non-returnable unless defective

## Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a return, items must:

1. Be in original, unused condition
2. Include all original packaging and accessories
3. Have proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)
4. Not be marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Log into your account at smartinsights.com/orders
2. Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
3. Click "Start Return" and select the reason
4. Print the prepaid shipping label
5. Pack items securely and drop off at any carrier location

## Refund Processing

- **Credit card refunds**: 5-7 business days after we receive the item
- **PayPal refunds**: 3-5 business days
- **Store credit**: Issued within 24 hours of processing

## Exceptions

### Non-Returnable Items
- Personalized or customized products
- Perishable goods
- Downloadable software or digital products
- Gift cards
- Items marked "Final Sale"

### Defective Items
If you received a defective or damaged item, please contact customer support within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund including shipping costs.

## Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the original item and place a new order for the desired product.

## International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective. Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable.
