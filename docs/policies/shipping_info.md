# Shipping Information

## Shipping Options

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs:

### Domestic Shipping (United States)

| Service | Delivery Time | Cost |
|---------|---------------|------|
| Standard Shipping | 5-7 business days | Free on orders $50+ |
| Standard Shipping | 5-7 business days | $5.99 (orders under $50) |
| Expedited Shipping | 2-3 business days | $12.99 |
| Next Day Delivery | 1 business day | $24.99 |
| Same Day Delivery | Same day (select areas) | $29.99 |

### International Shipping

| Region | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Canada | 7-10 business days | $14.99 |
| Europe | 10-14 business days | $19.99 |
| Asia Pacific | 12-18 business days | $24.99 |
| Rest of World | 14-21 business days | $29.99 |

## Carriers

We partner with trusted carriers to ensure reliable delivery:

- **DHL** - Primary carrier for domestic shipments
- **FedEx** - Expedited and international shipments
- **LocalCarrier** - Same-day and regional deliveries

## Order Processing

- Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on business days are processed same day
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST are processed next business day
- Weekend orders are processed on Monday

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive:
1. Email confirmation with tracking number
2. SMS updates (if opted in)
3. Real-time tracking at smartinsights.com/track

## Delivery Instructions

You can add special delivery instructions at checkout:
- Leave at door
- Require signature
- Deliver to neighbor
- Hold at carrier facility

## Address Requirements

Please ensure your shipping address includes:
- Full name (as it appears on ID for signature deliveries)
- Street address (no PO boxes for expedited shipping)
- Apartment/Suite number if applicable
- City, State, ZIP code
- Phone number for delivery updates

## Shipping Restrictions

Some items cannot be shipped to certain locations:
- Hazardous materials (batteries, chemicals)
- Oversized items to Alaska/Hawaii
- Certain electronics to international destinations

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged:
1. Report within 7 days of expected delivery
2. Contact support@smartinsights.com
3. We will file a claim and send a replacement or refund

## Holiday Shipping

During peak seasons (November-December), please allow extra time:
- Standard: Add 2-3 days
- Expedited: Add 1 day
- Order early to ensure holiday delivery
